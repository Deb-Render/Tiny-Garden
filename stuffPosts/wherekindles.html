Where Are all the Kindles?

I love my E-readers. I use both Kindle and Kobo but the Kindle is by far my favorite.

I have the 2018 Paperwhite and in the last few months, it’s become so slow and started re-booting at random. Ive been putting off an upgrade as I don’t like how big the newest generation Paperwhite is and the basic is missing some features.

I’ve done a lot of research in the past few weeks and eventually decided I need nothing more than the basic. I own an oasis which never leaves the house, so the basic would be ideal to throw in my bag and take when traveling, etc.

Ive searched everywhere and there are no kindles in stock!

So what is going on? A month ago they were all in stock on amazon and at our local electrical good store. The Kindle Scribe is the only one available, but at 10.2 inches and at a steep price, it’s just not for me.

There is speculation of two new kindles being released before the end of this year and in an article from Good e-Reader amazon have attained FCC certification for those.

There are also rumours of a color kindle Paperwhite being released, so could this be the reason for the current lack of stock?

Personally, I’m not convinced, there is obviously still a market for the 2022 kindle basic and the 2021 kindle Paperwhite, so why would amazon lose out on money intentionally?

They discontinued the Kindle Oasis earlier this year, so are they doing the same with the current models ahead of the next release?

Only time will tell.

If you want to stay up to date with kindle news, follow the Good e-Reader and checkout, the Kindle Subreddit.
