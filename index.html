Tiny Garden

Hi, I’m Deb. I’m teaching myself web development and want to document the process.

This is the third version of this site and hopefully the last. Ive left my Previous build online.

It was taking all my time trying to get it perfect when all I really wanted was a simple place on the web to document, share, make notes and ramble, etc.

I did some research to see what other people were doing and found (Tiny Projects) and loved the concept of a tiny website, so started again and kept it simple.

I realised that the place I'm documenting does not need to be pretty, with all the bells and whistles. I can showcase all that stuff on the projects I build, and my portfolio. Both are places I want to spend more time.
